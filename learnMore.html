Security Best Practices

Protect Your Keys

Guard your seed phrase with utmost care. Store it offline in a secure location and never share it with anyone. This is your most crucial security measure.

Hardware Security

Consider using a hardware wallet for substantial holdings. These physical devices provide maximum security by keeping your private keys offline.

Verify Everything

Always double-check transaction details, including recipient addresses and amounts. Take your time to review before confirming any transaction.

Stay Vigilant

Be wary of unexpected offers and suspicious links. Only connect your wallet to trusted platforms and verify all requests carefully.
